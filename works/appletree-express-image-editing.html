Appletree Express
Image Editing

Appletree Express is a website built for the restaurant in London, Ontario, called Appletree.  They took some images to post on the website when customers order, and I was asked to edit their images to make the imperfection go away.  They mainly wanted me to fix the lighting, marks, backgrounds, etc.

Before
After

For the restaurant’s Belgium fries, I had to use the regular and spicy mayo from one picture and paste it onto another.  

I also had to photoshop out the spoons that were sticking out of the mayo containers.

To finish off, I was requested to get rid of the background and make it a clear png before sending it off to the website developer.

After
