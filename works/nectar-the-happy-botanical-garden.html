Nectar: The Happy
Botanical Garden

The design for this website is fairly simple and minimalistic.  I tried to keep the theme of white a green throughout the website.

Instagram @ne.c.t.ar
visit site

process

I started off by collecting images from the garden for this website.  I am not a great photographer, but I did the best I could!

After, I chose the best ones and displayed them on the front page.  Since the theme of the blog is white and green, I decided to place the only white and green plant I found in the garden.

home page

After I collected the images, I then started to think about the logo.  Taking the pictures, I had some idea of what I wanted the logo to look like.  Since there were so many flowers in the garden, I decided to use a flower design for the logo.  I then narrowed my options down to 2, which also really directed what the overall website would look like.  I went with Option A.

Option A

This is a very simplistic design with very little detail.  I could imagine this type of logo used in more recent websites.  It gives the entire website a more modern and minimalistic look.

Option B

Personally, I liked this design more than option A; however, I thought that since I wanted the website to appear more sleek and modern, I opted out for option 1.  This option would have made the website look older mainly because of the font and the fine details on the flower.
