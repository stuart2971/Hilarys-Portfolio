Custom One Night
Werewolf Cards

A client of mine requested a custom deck of cards for the game “one night werewolf.” They wanted the cards to have the same roles as the original game, but all that is different is the decoration of what the characters look like.  My client requested to have characers from their favourite shows/movies to make the game more interesting.

I was given a list of characters they want for the game.  I ended up making about 3-4 different characters for each role.

As for the actual design for the cards, it was totally up to me. I decided to base them off Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh! and Dragon Shield cards, since I knew that my client mentioned that they are interested in them.

my process

For one of the troublemaker cards, I was required to use Team Rocket from the Japanese anime, Pokemon.  
I used a reference picture to redraw the image from scratch.  I decided to use this method because since I am printing these cards, I wanted the drawing to be the best quality.  

For some of the cards, I decided to simply “restore” them.  I did this by redrawing the character but in better quality, otherwise, they would be blurry or pixely.

For one of the troublemaker cards, I was required to use Team Rocket from the Japanese anime, Pokemon.  
I used a reference picture to redraw the image from scratch.  I decided to use this method because since I am printing these cards, I wanted the drawing to be the best quality.  

For some of the cards, I decided to simply “restore” them.  I did this by redrawing the character but in better quality, otherwise, they would be blurry or pixely.

For the logo/backside of the card, I tried to keep it very simplistic yet recognizable.  

After several drafts, I decided to go with a werewolf head silhouette and a bright, yellow moon shining behind it.  

printing

The final size of the cards ended up being the same size as an ordinary baseball card (6.3cm x 8.8cm).  

After printing the designs on card stock, with the logo on the back of each card, I decided to place them in replaceable card sleeves to protect them from any damage.
